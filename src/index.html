Pritong Itlog Pritong Hotdog Instant Noodles

Pritong Itlog

Ingredients

  1. Eggs
  2. Salt
  3. Pepper
  4. Cooking oil

Procedure

  1. Put some oil in the pan and put it on low heat
  2. Crack eggs into the pan
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper to your liking
  4. Enjoy!
